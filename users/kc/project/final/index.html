Who Am I?

Hello! My name is KC Guzman. I am a student at the University of Northern Colorado, studying Software Engineering and music. I love doing creative things and expanding my abilities through practice and having fun. I am known as Spiral Harmonies online, or to many of my followers, simply as "Duk".

What do I do?

As a creative mind, one of the things I am best known for is music. I love making music for both myself, and others to hear. I love making scores for things such as animation, radio plays, or the like. Being compliment to peices such as these is an amazing feeling when everything finally comes together in a large project, and I love being able to give feeling to a project through the expression of orchestration.
